SAVE AN ANGEL WEBSITE

Responsive Redesign for a Non-Profit Organization

THE PROBLEM

Save An Angel is a pet rescue organization that requires the help of the community to fuel its mission efforts. There is a high interest in getting involved in different areas of the animal rescue mission, including volunteering, adopting, fostering, donating and supporting fundraising events, but users are less likely to help as the required effort increases or resource availability decreases. Therefore, users need a way to give back with minimal effort and roadblocks.

STAKEHOLDER MEETING TAKEAWAYS

Their main market is women aged roughly 45-55 years, who are tech-savy enough to find their way around facebook.

Most people access the website through mobile. Their current site is outdated and not responsive to screen size.

Save An Angel’s advertisement is most successful when it pulls at the heartstrings. Imagery evoking compassion and empathy pulls the users in.

Most all of their funding comes from individual donations, and they rely on many volunteers to execute their mission.
